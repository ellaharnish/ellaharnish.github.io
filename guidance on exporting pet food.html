Guidance on exporting pet food

This work began when New Zealand was seeing a rise in pet food exporters. Exporters told us that the process was complicated and difficult to understand.
We worked with Pet Food exporters to create resources to help them on their exporting journey. It was important that the resources were engaging, written in plain English, and catered to different learning styles.

The animated video below gives an overview of the things you need to think about before exporting pet food. I created the aniamtion and voice over and helped with the script writing.





The pet food glossary helps to define common terms and classifications.



The decision tree makes it easier for exporters to know which rules apply based on the type of pet food and the markets they are exporting to.
