Organics

This project involved creating material for workshops and hui on the Organics Legislation, inviting the organic sector to discuss the possibilities and to have their say. I am helping to facilitate the workshops which are currently taking place across New Zealand.

See below a series of infographics that describe the context around the Organics bill. At the time of creation, due to the uncertainty of Covid 19, the material needed to be adaptable to fit both a digital and physical space.


We discovered that the material needed to be altered to work best with the round table discussion (below).


We explored different ways to visually show the proposed process for domestic organic producers of different sizes, as well as exporters and importers.
