Organics

This project involved creating material for walkthroughs on the Organics Legislation, inviting the organic sector and public to learn about the possibilities and to have their say. Due to the uncertaintly of Covid 19, the meterial needed to be adaptable to fit both a digital and physical space. The majority of the this work was created with the team working remotely using Miro.

See below a series of infographics that describe the context around the Organics bill. These have gone through multiple iterations, testing content, design style, and tone.


We explored different ways to visually show the proposed process for domestic organic producers of different sizes, as well as exporters and importers.
