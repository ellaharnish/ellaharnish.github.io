SituateMe

Working with a talented group of developers, designers and entrepreneurs at the Start-Up Weekend Wellington, we worked on an idea to help with humanitarian assistance and disaster relief. After 54 hours, we pitched our idea to a judging panel and came in first.

Our idea is an empathetic chatbot called Ema that engages with civilians in the event of a disaster to gather information. This provides decision makers with a dashboard enabling them to make choices quicker and save more lives.

I worked on user experience, visual design, journey mapping, and testing the chatbot to create friendly and compassionate responses in the event of a disaster. I also helped out with marketing and managing the social media accounts.
