SituateMe

Ema, an empathetic chatbot for disaster response, was initially created during Start-Up Weekend Wellington, where our concept won first place. After the event, our team continued to develop Ema into a working prototype to help civilians share real-time information during emergencies, enabling faster, more informed decisions by responders.

Ema engages directly with affected individuals to gather vital, real-time information, which is then fed into a dashboard to support faster, more informed decision-making by emergency responders.

My role focused on designing the conversation flow for Ema, ensuring the bot delivered clear, compassionate, and human-centred responses in high-stress scenarios. I also contributed to the user experience design, journey mapping, interface testing, and visual design of the chatbot. In addition, I supported our outreach efforts through marketing and managing the project's social media presence.
