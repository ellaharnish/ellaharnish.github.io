Te Kete Haumaru Kai

Te Kete Haumaru Kai (The Food Safety Kit) aims to empower marae in the Far North to enter the commercial setting of hosting marae stays for manuhiri. The Kete contains bilingual designed resources detailing the food rules and requirements. Inside the kete are booklets, a flipchart, videos, posters, stickers and magnets.

The concept of weaving, captured in the tukutuku designs, is a reflection of spiritual values in Te Ao Māori. It also visually captures interwoven relationships such as marae with New Zealand Food Safety, tikanga with legislation, manaakitanga with business and intergenerational knowledge sharing.
