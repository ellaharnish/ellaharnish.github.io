MEGT Strategy publication

The MEGT Strategy publication presents the new business strategy: Broadening Horizons. I worked with another designer to create a visual identity that brought Broadening Horizons to life, whilst keeping to the MEGT brand guidelines.

The circles in the Broadening Horizons logo represent directional growth and upward progression for the business and are repeated throughout the document. The fans from the original MEGT logo are overlayed on photos of smiling apprentices and trainees, showing MEGT's positive impact and future aspirations.
