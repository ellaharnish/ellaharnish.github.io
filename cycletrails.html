Pou Herenga Tai Marae stays

This is an ongoing project, working with Marae in the Far North to empower them to enter the commercial setting of hosting marae stays for manuhiri.
We are currently running workshops and testing sessions with different marae in the area, helping us to understand their traditional processes and Tikanga in relation to kai. Our design team are creating and testing a food safety plan and a kete of guidance material for the marae based on our findings.

It also visually captures many interwoven relationships such as marae with New Zealand Food Safety, tikanga with legislation, manaakitanga with business, and intergenerational knowledge sharing.
