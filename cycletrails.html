Pou Herenga Tai - Cycle Trail

This is an ongoing project, working with Marae in the Far North to provide food for visitors cycling the Po Herenga Tai cycle trail.
We are currently running workshops and testing sessions with different marae in the area, helping us to understand their traditional processes and Tikanga in relation to kai. We are creating and testing a food safety plan for the marae based on our findings.
