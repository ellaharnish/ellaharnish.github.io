Hodge Podge

Hodge Podge is a project which aims to reduce food waste within a domestic setting. The website was created with a user centred approach with millennials as a focus audience.

Food sharing is facilitated among flatmates, neighbours and community members focusing on using up food scraps and leftovers rather than throwing them away to create delicious shared meals. The website shows the positive impact being made personally and within the community by saving food, which motivates users to continue to use up their food scraps.
