Kia ora, I’m Ella!

I am enthusiastic about creating meaningful and enjoyable experiences through engaging visual communication design.

I enjoy projects that are driven by a deep understanding of the behaviour, experiences, and challenges of the end user. With this understanding, I believe design can create positive change within our society.

I am a conscientious and approachable person, bringing compassion and empathy to design projects as I value the experience of the end user. I thrive in a team environment and enjoy helping others.

I am always eager to learn through challenging experiences that broaden my skill set. Design empowers me as I can create solutions to real world problems whilst being creative and innovative.
