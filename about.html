Hello! I’m Ella.

I am a visual communication designer driven by the belief that design can and should create positive social change. Currently, as a Lead Designer, I’ve expanded my focus from individual execution to strategic oversight. This role has taught me that the most impactful designs come from a place of radical empathy, not just for the end-user, but for the team creating the solution.

I pride myself on being conscientious and approachable, having learned through my role as a Lead Designer that the most effective solutions to real-world problems are born from a culture of clear communication and genuine support.
