Tātou

Being close to someone while knowing their life will end soon is hard. Experiencing loss is a daily reality for hospice nurses.
Tātou is a service for hospice nurses that facilitates self-care to enhance their wellbeing and resilience. Tātou helps hospice nurses navigate the difficulties of compassion fatigue by making self-care accessible, both individually and with peers.
