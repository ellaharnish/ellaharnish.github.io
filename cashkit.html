My Cash Kit

My Cash Kit is an accessible toolkit which presents an engaging and empowering way for year 9 and 10 New Zealand students to learn about financial capability in a relatable, safe, and supportive environment. The digital game is designed to be supplementary to the school curriculum.
My Cash Kit Year financially equips year 9 and 10 students before they enter the real world, as students can legally leave school after they turn 16 years old.
I worked in a team of three on this start up project, in which we contributed equal effort.
