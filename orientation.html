Massey Orientation Guide

I collaborated on this project as part of a team while employed at Open Lab, Massey University. My role included design and layout in Adobe InDesign and editing images in Photoshop and Illustrator. The project allowed me creative freedom and playfulness, while keeping to the College of Creative Arts brand guidelines. The Massey Orientation Guide provides first-year and new students with study tips and advice, support, program information, and a large map of the university and surrounding area.
